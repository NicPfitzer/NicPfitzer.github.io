Automata-Based Reasoning and Collaboration for Robot Teams

ProrokLab – University of Cambridge

LLM-guided tasking system using recurrent policies and automata execution to coordinate multi-robot behaviors from high-level language commands.

Our framework, called CARTELL, distils reasoning from large language models into lightweight recurrent automata. These internal states are then used to condition graph-based policies that let robots act in real-time with only local information. The result is distributed collaboration and reasoning that runs onboard, enabling teams of robots to carry out complex multi-stage tasks from a single natural language prompt.

Evaluations in simulation and on RoboMaster platforms demonstrate that the approach achieves correct automata transitions, robust collaboration under noisy conditions, and even zero-shot deployments where the team adapts to new task descriptions without retraining.
